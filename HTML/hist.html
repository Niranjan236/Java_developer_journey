Historical Sites

 In ancient stones, history lies,
        Whispers of tales that reach the skies.
        Standing strong through ages gone by,
        These sites evoke a heartfelt sigh.
        
        From pyramids to castles old,
        The stories of the past they hold.
        Ruins of empires, echoes of time,
        In these places, history's sublime.
        
        Where footsteps of the ancients tread,
        Legends of the past, now widespread.
        In the hallowed halls of yesteryears,
        The weight of history truly appears.
