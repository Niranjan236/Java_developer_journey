Goa



Infinite depths of blue, a boundless sea,
        Where waves dance with joyful glee.
        The ocean's song, a soothing melody,
        A world of wonder, vast and free.
        
        Beneath the surface, secrets hide,
        Coral gardens and creatures side by side.
         Majestic whales and dolphins glide,                                   
       
        In this liquid realm, they gracefully bide.
        So let us cherish this wondrous place,
        The ocean's beauty, its awe and grace.
        For in its depths, we find a trace,
        Of nature's magic, a precious embrace.
